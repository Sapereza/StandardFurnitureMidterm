About Us

In 1975 my father bought a wearhouse in western Bristol, by the Bristol Channel. It was there he started his export of handmade furniture. Eventually the business grew to be a top wholesaler of furniture in Europe and abroad.

Today I, Brent Taylor, run this thriving company after being handed down to me from my father. I plan ensure its success for many more years to come.
