  • About Us

Started in 1995 with just three employees, Standard Furniture(makers of European style furniture) grew to become a leading national furniture wholesaler. Today, it caters to over several thousands customers and enjoys exclusive distribution rights from leading European manufacturers such as Camoso, Dugal, Hosmer & Brent and so on.

We maintain vast stock at our South Boston warehouse to ensure fast and reliable distribution in the USA and Canada.

In addition, Standard Furniture offers a comprehensive special order program from our vendors. We pride ourselves with partnering with the best furniture manufacturers world-wide to bring our customers a thoughtful selection across all product categories and price ranges.

The breadth of the Standard Furniture partner network guarantees we will have a product to satisfy the most exquisite customer's needs...

We are committed to continuous innovation and improvement in our products and services. All Standard Furniture merchandise is checked meticulously by our professional Quality Control Team. We also offer replacement parts and repair services.

Our trained Customer Service Representatives are ready to help you with any questions. Customer Support working hours are Monday through Friday 10-6pm Eastern Standard Time, with inventory information available 24 hours on our website.
